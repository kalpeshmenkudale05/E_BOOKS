Book Description

Josephine's blind date becomes unforgettable when billionaire Holden Winters unexpectedly rescues her. She never imagined someone from the powerful Winters family would notice an ordinary graduate student.

Holden is used to getting everything he wants, but Josephine is different. Intelligent, independent, and impossible to forget, she becomes the one woman who refuses to chase his wealth or fame.

A romantic journey filled with passion, secrets, and unexpected love, this novella begins the bestselling Winters Saga.
